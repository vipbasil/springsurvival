# Canon Memory

## Stable Facts

- Humanity mainly survives on the Ark, a small orbital refuge.
- The Ark has only about 500-600 people left.
- At around 40 years old, a person must choose between becoming biomass for the Ark or descending to Earth as an operator.
- Operators are sent to build hidden shelters, survive quietly, preserve knowledge, and improve the odds for later operators.
- This has been happening for hundreds of years with very little success.
- Earth is still under persistent orbital machine surveillance.
- Survival depends on low-signature systems: quiet, low-energy, low-waste, hard to detect.
- The larger hope is that enough operators might survive, reconnect, and eventually make Earth habitable for people again.

## Tone

- quiet
- fragile
- rationed
- patient
- tense
- unsentimental

## Anti-Drift

- Do not flatten the setting into generic post-apocalypse scavenger fiction.
- Do not frame the Ark as a thriving heroic command ship.
- Do not treat stealth as optional flavor.
- Do not treat knowledge as secondary to material accumulation.
